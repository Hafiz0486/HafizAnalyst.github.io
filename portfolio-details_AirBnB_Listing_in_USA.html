AirBnB Listing in USA

Airbnb, Inc. is a San Francisco-based company that operates an online marketplace for travel information and booking services. Their platform primarily offers private rooms, primary homes, or vacation homes for short-term homestays and experiences. As a sales department, it is crucial to utilize available Airbnb data such as the total number of hosts, listings, average reviews, average prices, and other relevant information to make informed decisions and drive revenue growth. By leveraging this data and insights, the sales team can enhance their decision-making accuracy and identify effective strategies to increase Airbnb sales in the USA.
