Supermarket Sales Dashboard

              

                As a junior data analyst in a big supermarket, I created a two-page dashboard to analyze supermarket transactions and presented it to the executive board. The first page featured charts displaying the relationship between invoice ID and gross income by month and day, average rating by product category, and gross income by product category. On the second page, I presented charts showing the average gross income per quantity by product category, quantity by product category, and gross income by quantity. The dashboard provided valuable insights into revenue trends, customer preferences, and top-selling product categories, enabling data-driven decision-making for revenue optimization and improved customer experience in the supermarket.
